About
======

I am a Senior Economist at Bates White Economics Consulting in the Antitrust practice. I received my Economics PhD (trade, labor) from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2023. 

Outside of Bates White, I am involved in the carillon community as a performer and volunteer, serving as a director of the Guild of Carillonneurs of North America and based at the Netherlands Carillon in Arlington, VA and the Basilica of the National Shrine of the Immaculate Conception in Washington, DC.
